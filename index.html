#OnOf

This is culture shock time. This is years of watching Gordie shore, leading us to believe everyone and everything on the shore is reality and Newcatle will be a reflection of this.  This is the same as comparing us down here to good all EastEnders I know, but don't take the surprise from us just yet. 

We are all 30 something's, most with kids or wives. Weekends are normally filled with MoTD and abusive texts at each other, with the occasional pub trip or even rarer trip to a club.

This website, should be treated like a historical document.  This is the lead up to the last night out of my life I am not going to be a married man.  To those of you whom have gone through this and have come out the other side intact, kudos to you.

I have had trips to Magaluf, weekends away and crazy nights out all the time I have been with my beautiful fiancee.  This should n't be different yet some how it is.  I feel like a puppy on the way to the vets, I am jubilant at the moment and have just been nodding my head and enjoying the engagement vibe.  Soon though my balls will be clipped, and sure enough I will have a very happy life,  but a ball less one.

Let the journey begin……………
